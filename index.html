How To Play

Time scale: 1 second = 1 hour. If you go 7 days without food you die. If you go 3 days without water you die.

Move: WASD or arrow keys. Eat nearby food: E. Drink nearby water: R. Strike: K. Craft: C. Trade: T. Bless at shrine: B.

Hints: food and water keep you alive, fullness warns against overeating, merchants and shrines support the peaceful path, and 18 warriors of each trait compete for resources around you.
